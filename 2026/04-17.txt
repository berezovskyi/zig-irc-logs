I mean, I'm in that boat too, but I get we're in a transitional phase and there's more to come on the std.Io front.

1776436236

there will probably always be edge cases where some corner-case high-performance need means that you drop down to std.posix.system.foo() and/or even std.os.linux.foo() and/or direct use of e.g. std.os.linux.IoUring, std.posix.system.kqueue, etc

1776436302

1776436525
